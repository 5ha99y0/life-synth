Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

TL;DR: Life Synth processes everything in your browser. We never see your keystrokes, mouse movements, or the music you create. The only data we handle is payment info through Stripe.

1. What We Collect

We collect the absolute minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • Account data: Email address (for Premium subscribers, via Stripe)
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store credit card numbers.
  • Analytics: Basic page views and session counts (no personally identifiable information)

2. What We DO NOT Collect

Life Synth is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • We do NOT record or transmit your keystrokes
  • We do NOT track your mouse movements
  • We do NOT store any behavioral data
  • We do NOT record the audio generated during sessions
  • We do NOT use cookies for tracking

All behavioral data processing and audio synthesis occurs entirely within your browser using the Web Audio API. No behavioral data ever leaves your device.

3. Premium Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed through Stripe. When you subscribe, Stripe collects your payment information directly. We receive only confirmation of your subscription status. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

4. Exported Recordings

Premium users can export session recordings. These recordings are created locally in your browser and downloaded directly to your device. We never receive, process, or store your exported audio files.

5. LocalStorage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember your Premium status and preferences. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Vercel: Website hosting
  • Google Fonts: Typography (Outfit, JetBrains Mono)

7. Data Retention

Since we don't collect behavioral data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Subscription data is retained as long as your subscription is active, plus 30 days after cancellation.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any data we hold (email us)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Clear localStorage in your browser to remove local preferences

9. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@lifesynth.app
