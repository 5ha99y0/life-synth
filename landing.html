How It Works

Your behavior is the instrument.

Every digital action generates a unique sound. Combined, they create a composition that is entirely, unmistakably yours.

Mouse Movement

Spatial Sound

Your cursor traces melodies across a spatial soundstage. Speed controls velocity, position controls panning and particle density.

Typing Rhythm

Melodic Patterns

Each key triggers a note from a dynamic scale. Your typing rhythm defines the tempo. Fast bursts create arpeggios; pauses create rests.

Clicking

Percussion

Clicks become percussive hits - snares, kicks, and hi-hats. Click intensity and frequency shape the rhythmic energy of your composition.

Going Idle

Ambient Drift

Step away and the music doesn't stop. It evolves into cosmic ambient pads, shimmering sparkles, and deep space drone textures.
